# Saudi / GCC Legal Glossary

Use these consistently across every document translated in **legal mode**. When a term is not listed here, pick a rendering, use it throughout, and record the choice in the translator's note.

## Numbers, Dates, and Currencies

- **Hijri dates**: keep as written, e.g. `7/8/1447 AH`. Add Gregorian equivalent in square brackets with `≈` to flag approximation: `7/8/1447 AH [≈ 26 January 2026]`. List every conversion in the translator's note.
- **Gregorian dates in source**: copy as-is.
- **Saudi Riyal amounts**: render as `SAR <amount>` in English, `沙特里亚尔 <金额>` in Chinese. Do not convert to other currencies unless the user asks.
- **Reference numbers** (case numbers, decision numbers, CR numbers, license numbers): copy verbatim, never normalize. Arabic-Indic digits (٠١٢٣٤٥٦٧٨٩) may be transcribed to Western digits (0123456789) for readability in EN/ZH output, but flag the transcription choice in the translator's note and keep the original form in the Arabic column.

## Conventional Abbreviations

When the source uses an Arabic abbreviation, give the full Arabic form on first mention, then the English/Chinese rendering in parentheses, then use the abbreviation thereafter.

- CMA — Capital Market Authority / 资本市场局
- SAIP — Saudi Authority for Intellectual Property / 沙特知识产权局
